# Business Recommendations

## Strategic Priority 1: Increase Repeat-Customer Retention
**Why:** Repeat customers show higher value and should be protected early.

**Recommended actions**
- Launch tiered loyalty benefits linked to repeat behavior.
- Trigger personalized offers after first and second purchases.
- Run re-engagement automations for lapse-risk customers.

**Suggested KPI tracking**
- Repeat Customer %
- Average Repeat LTV
- 30/60/90-day reorder rate

---

## Strategic Priority 2: Protect and Grow VIP Customers
**Why:** Revenue concentration indicates a small customer segment contributes disproportionately.

**Recommended actions**
- Introduce VIP-exclusive offers and early-access campaigns.
- Provide premium support pathways for top-value accounts.
- Apply account-level CRM treatment and targeted outreach.

**Suggested KPI tracking**
- Top 10% revenue share
- VIP retention rate
- VIP AOV and order frequency trend

---

## Strategic Priority 3: Upsell Frequent, Lower-AOV Customers
**Why:** Some customers purchase often but spend modestly per order.

**Recommended actions**
- Bundle frequently co-purchased products.
- Deploy cross-sell recommendations at cart/checkout.
- Test premium product recommendations for likely adopters.

**Suggested KPI tracking**
- AOV uplift among high-frequency segment
- Bundle attachment rate
- Revenue per order trend

---

## Strategic Priority 4: Re-engage Low-Frequency Customers
**Why:** Infrequent customers represent recoverable value with targeted nudges.

**Recommended actions**
- Build win-back campaigns by inactivity window.
- Offer first-repeat incentives with controlled discounting.
- Personalize reminders based on category affinity.

**Suggested KPI tracking**
- Win-back conversion rate
- First-repeat conversion rate
- Incremental revenue from reactivation cohorts
